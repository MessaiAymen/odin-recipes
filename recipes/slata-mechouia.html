Slata Mechouia

Slata Mechouia is a tunisian traditional salad made from grilled vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Green pepper
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Spices

Cooking tips

  1. If you prefer a non-spicy version of this dish, simply omit the chili in your Roasted Vegetable Mechouia salad.
  2. If your Mechouia is too dry, feel free to add more olive oil before serving.
  3. To make this recipe gluten-free, use certified gluten-free food products where possible. You can also use regular wheat bread for dipping instead of pita bread.
